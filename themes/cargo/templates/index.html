S. Cargo (beta)

Someone's stolen the postie's shell!

Join Cargo in the adventure, exploring the world from a snail's point of view! Solve puzzles and collect items to work your way to the top of the tree, where the nefarious Bird awaits.

S. Cargo is an epic 3D adventure game for GNU/Linux and Windows. A Mac version is coming soon!

Cargo lives on a quiet island, working as a postsnail. She is excellent at her job. She has been known to sleep in a little, but no one really expects fast deliveries. Life is good — until one day, when things start disappearing from the island. Now she must take part in a desperate race to get back the things that were stolen.

About

This game was designed for people aged 8-13, but adults are sure to enjoy it too. There is no violence in this game — but you might be stung by a bee.

S. Cargo is an independent free software 3D adventure game. If you are curious, you can read about how it was made.

Free software doesn't mean "free of charge". We ask that you buy a copy of the game. Free software actually means that this game respects your freedom. You can use the game in any way you like, study the source code, modify the game and share it with your friends.
