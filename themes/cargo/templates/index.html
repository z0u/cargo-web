Someone's stolen the postie's shell!

Join Cargo in the adventure, exploring the world from a snail's point of view! Solve puzzles and collect items to work your way to the top of the tree, where the nefarious Bird awaits.

Cargo lives on a quiet island, working as a postsnail. She is excellent at her job. She has been known to sleep in a little, but no one really expects fast deliveries. Life is good - until one day, when things start disappearing from the island. Now, she must take part in a desperate race to get back the things that were stolen.

Cargo is free software

Free software doesn't mean "free of charge". We ask you to buy a copy of the game. Free software actually means that this game respects your freedom. It does this by endorsing your rights to use the game in any way you like, study the source code, modify the game and share it with your friends. These are fundamental values of the free software movement; a movement for the freedom of people who use software.

Unlike many games, Cargo is also free of DRM and other technical restrictions.
